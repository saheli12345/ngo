About Our NGO


Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs), also known as non-profit organizations or civil society organizations, are entities that operate independently of government control and are typically established to address various social, environmental, and humanitarian issues. NGOs play a crucial role in addressing societal challenges and advocating for positive change. NGOs contribute significantly to social and environmental progress globally, but like any organizations, they can vary in terms of their effectiveness and impact. Due diligence is essential when supporting or working with NGOs to ensure that they align with your values and objectives.
